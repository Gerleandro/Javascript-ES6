Fantastic Beasts

Tiger

The Asian tiger, recognized for its orange fur and black stripes, faces significant risks of extinction due to illegal hunting and habitat loss. Males can weigh over 300 kg, emphasizing their imposing presence.

Global conservation initiatives, such as reserves and captive breeding programs, aim to protect these felines.

In addition to their ecological importance, tigers hold deep cultural significance in various Asian societies, being regarded as symbols of strength and courage.

Preserving these animals is crucial for maintaining biodiversity and the rich cultural heritage associated with them.

Fox

Foxes are mammalian and omnivorous animals belonging to the Canidae family. They are medium-sized vulpids, characterized by a long snout and a long, hairy tail

They also have their oval pupils, similar to the vertical pupils of felids
